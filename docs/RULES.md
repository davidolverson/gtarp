# RULES — gtarp roleplay

These rules are pinned in Discord. Breaking them is grounds for warning,
kick, or ban depending on severity and recurrence.

## 1. Core conduct

1.1. **Stay in character.** Use `/me` and `/do` for actions and details.
Break character only via `/ooc` when strictly necessary.

1.2. **No metagaming.** Information learned out of character cannot be
acted on in character. Discord and Twitch chat are out-of-character.

1.3. **No powergaming.** Your character is not invulnerable, super-fast,
or all-knowing. Lose with grace.

1.4. **Value of life applies to everyone**, including criminals. If a gun
is on you and complying is rational, comply.

## 2. RP framework

2.1. **New Life Rule (NLR).** After a hospital respawn you have forgotten
the events that led to your death.

2.2. **Fear RP.** Negotiate or comply if pulling a fight would get your
character killed.

2.3. **No cop-baiting.** Don't antagonize police for content with no
narrative payoff.

## 3. Public-server hygiene

3.1. **No racism, slurs, sexual content involving minors, or real-world
harassment.** Instant ban.

3.2. **No exploits, hacks, or third-party tools.** Reporting an exploit
in Discord is fine; using one is a ban.

3.3. **Respect staff.** Disagreements go to a ticket, not in-character
yelling at moderators.

## 4. Reporting

- In-game: `/report <message>`
- Discord: ticket channel pinned at the top of the server

## 5. Whitelist & allowlist

- General membership: open allowlist via Discord application.
- Emergency-services roles (police, EMS): separate whitelist, see Discord
  application form. Enforced by `gtarp_whitelist_jobs`.

Repeat violations of any rule above can result in removal from both
allowlists.

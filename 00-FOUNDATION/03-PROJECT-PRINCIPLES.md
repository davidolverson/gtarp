# Palm6 Project Principles

**Version:** v0.1.0  
**Status:** Draft — Under Review

---

These are the permanent principles that guide every decision on Palm6.

**Principle 01 — Restraint over decoration**  
We add details because they strengthen the experience, not because we can.

**Principle 02 — Brand before feature**  
Every feature, asset, and system must reinforce the Palm6 identity first.

**Principle 03 — One visual language**  
Everything should feel like it came from the same creative studio, even if created at different times.

**Principle 04 — Luxury over loudness**  
We aim for premium, cinematic quality rather than flashy or generic roleplay aesthetics.

**Principle 05 — Cinematic marketing, timeless identity**  
System B (Marketing) can be rich and emotional. System A (Identity) must remain clean and timeless.

**Principle 06 — Everything earns its place**  
If something does not clearly support the North Star or Creative System, it does not ship.

**Principle 07 — Design for ten years, not ten days**  
We build with longevity in mind. Trends come and go. Palm6 should feel coherent for years.

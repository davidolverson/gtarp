# Palm6 Quality Standards

**Version:** v1.0.0  
**Status:** Approved v1.0.0 (Phase 0, DEC-004, 2026-07-18)

---

Every asset created for Palm6 must pass these standards before it can be considered for approval.

## Core Quality Questions

Before anything ships, ask:

- Does it feel like Palm6?
- Would it look good on a luxury hoodie or engraved on a wallet?
- Could it appear on the side of a yacht or performance car?
- Would Rockstar’s art team consider it premium?
- Can it be recognized at 32px?
- Does it work in one color (solid black on white)?
- Is it original and ownable?
- Does it rely on effects (glow, heavy textures, dramatic lighting) to look good?
- Would it still look good five years from now?
- Does it strengthen the Palm6 universe?

If the answer to any of these is unclear or negative, the asset needs refinement.

## Asset Status Lifecycle

Every asset must move through these stages:

- **🟡 Experimental** - Early concepts and explorations.
- **🟠 Candidate** - Under formal review against the Creative System.
- **🟢 Approved** - Passed review and moved into the Vault.
- **⚫ Archived** - Retired but kept for historical reference.

Nothing moves into the Vault until it reaches **Approved** status.

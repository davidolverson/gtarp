# Palm6 Design Manifesto

**Version:** v1.0.0  
**Status:** Approved v1.0.0 (Phase 0, DEC-004, 2026-07-18)  
**Purpose:** This is the first document anyone should read before contributing to Palm6.

---

Palm6 is not another GTA roleplay server.

We are building a premium, immersive, and believable modern crime roleplay universe with the same level of craftsmanship, consistency, and long-term thinking as world-class entertainment brands and game studios.

We believe that roleplay deserves better than generic assets, inconsistent branding, and short-term trends. Every logo, UI screen, environment, vehicle livery, piece of clothing, and marketing asset should feel like it belongs to the same cohesive world - one that players want to belong to for years, not weeks.

**We value:**

- **Restraint over decoration**  
  We add details because they strengthen the experience, not because we can.

- **Timelessness over trends**  
  We design for longevity. If it would still look good in five years, it’s worth making.

- **Cohesion over novelty**  
  Everything should feel like it came from the same creative studio - even if created months or years apart.

- **Quality over quantity**  
  We would rather ship one exceptional asset than ten average ones.

- **Player belonging over spectacle**  
  The goal is not just to look impressive. It is to make players feel like they are part of something real and meaningful.

Palm6 exists to raise the standard.

Not for other roleplay servers.

For ourselves.

If a decision does not strengthen the Palm6 universe, it does not ship.

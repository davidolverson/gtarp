# Palm6 Brand Guidelines

**Version:** v0.1.0  
**Status:** Approved (v1.0.0, via DEC-002; frozen in 15-VAULT/)

## Overview

Palm6 uses a **Dual Visual System**:

- **System A (Identity)**: Clean, minimal, timeless, luxury. Used for logos, official marks, and core branding.
- **System B (Marketing)**: Cinematic, atmospheric, high-production. Used for trailers, key art, social media, and emotional storytelling.

## Core Rules

- The logo itself must always follow **System A** (never rely on effects, glows, or heavy textures).
- Marketing assets can be rich and cinematic (System B).
- Everything must pass the Quality Standards checklist.

## Logo Usage

- Primary wordmark and P6 monogram must work in solid black on white.
- Maintain generous negative space.
- Do not stretch, add effects, or place on busy backgrounds.

## Tone & Personality

Sophisticated. Cinematic. Confident. Premium but not cold. Serious but welcoming.

## Do’s and Don’ts

**Do:**
- Use restraint
- Prioritize timelessness and scalability
- Test at small sizes and in one color

**Don’t:**
- Make the logo carry all the emotion
- Use heavy effects on identity assets
- Follow short-term trends

# Palm6 Private-Business Logo System — Redo Brief

Version: v0.9.0-rc.1 (working direction)
Status: Direction only. Art is generated by David in ChatGPT; this doc is the brief +
ready-to-paste prompts. The first synthwave "P6" business batch was rejected and is NOT

---

## Why the first batch read as "AI slop"
1. **No system.** Every logo used a different loud neon rainbow gradient, so 20 marks
   looked like 20 unrelated Fiverr logos instead of one city's businesses.
2. **Dated gamer/esports look** — chrome, 3D bevels, thick drop shadows.
3. **The palm-tree-sunset was in every single one** → repetitive and generic.
4. **Over-detailed**, no restraint, no simple icon that survives at 24px.
5. Inconsistent "P6" badge tacked on.

## The target system (make them look expensive + cohesive)
- **One palette across all businesses.** Vary hue by industry within it; never a full
  rainbow per logo. Starting palette:
  - Midnight navy `#0B1B2B` (base) · Sunset coral `#FF6B4A` · Warm gold `#E8B04B` ·
    Teal `#1FA6A0` · Off-white `#F5F1E6`. Use **2–3 per logo**, not all five.
- **Flat / semi-flat vector.** No chrome, no 3D bevel, no drop shadow. Optional subtle
  grain only.
- **One simple icon per business** — a mark readable at small sizes (a real symbol, not
  a detailed illustration) — paired with a **clean wordmark in one consistent type
  family** (a confident geometric or condensed grotesk; pick ONE and reuse it).
- **Consistent lockup:** icon centered above the wordmark (or icon left, wordmark right),
  consistent padding. Same construction every time.
- **Drop the palm-sunset crutch.** Allow one subtle shared motif (e.g. a small palm mark
  or a horizon line) used sparingly as a system signature, not the whole logo.
- **Transparent background, square canvas, centered, balanced negative space.**
- Retro-modern Miami/Vice feel is fine, but **restrained and editorial**, not neon-vomit.

## Reusable ChatGPT prompt template
Paste this and fill the brackets:

> Design a **flat vector logo** for **[BUSINESS NAME]**, a **[industry]** in a modern
> Miami-inspired coastal city. Style: clean, premium, minimal, **flat vector — no 3D
> bevel, no chrome, no drop shadow, no gradient soup**. **One simple iconic symbol** of
> **[icon motif]**, paired with a **bold condensed geometric sans-serif wordmark**.
> Palette: use only 2–3 of {midnight navy `#0B1B2B`, sunset coral `#FF6B4A`, warm gold
> `#E8B04B`, teal `#1FA6A0`, off-white `#F5F1E6`}. Transparent background, square, centered,
> lots of negative space, **scalable and legible at small sizes**. It must sit in a
> cohesive city brand family. **No palm-tree-sunset scene.** Icon above wordmark, even padding.

Keep the **style/palette/lockup words identical** every time; only change the name, industry,
and icon motif. That is what makes them a family.

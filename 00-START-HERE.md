## Key Rules

- **System A (Identity)**: Minimal, timeless, ownable. Must work in one color and at small sizes.
- **System B (Marketing)**: Cinematic and atmospheric. Supports the identity but never replaces it.
- All assets follow: **Experimental → Candidate → Approved → Vault**
- Only **Approved** items go into the Vault.
- Propose changes via **RFC** (see 19-RFC folder).

## What You Can and Cannot Change

- You **may** propose improvements through the RFC process.
- You **may not** unilaterally change approved brand elements, folder structure, or governance rules without approval.
